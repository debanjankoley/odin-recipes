Chilli-Chicken

Description

Indo Chinese is very popular in India and one of the most loved is Chilli Chicken. This is one dish along with Chilli Paneer that cuts across all of India. Chilli Chicken is a spicy, lightly sweet appetizer that you can cook at home. Crispy marinated chicken is saut√©ed in a spicy-sweet sauce along with onions and bell peppers

This Chilli Chicken can be made as a dry appetizer or as a gravy to enjoy with Chinese style fried rice or Hakka noodles. Chilli chicken is a popular Indo-Chinese dish of chicken of Hakka Chinese heritage. In India, this may include a variety of dry chicken preparations. Though mainly boneless chicken is used in this dish, some people also use bone-in chicken too.

Ingredients

  • Dry kashmiri red chilli (soaked)
  • Boneless Chicken
  • Salt
  • Pepper powder
  • Baking soda
  • Maida
  • Cornstarch
  • Egg - 1
  • Oil
  • Dry chillies (optional)
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Green chillies
  • Onions
  • Capsicum
  • Soya-sauce
  • Ketchup
  • Vinegar
  • Spring Onions

Steps

  1. First, we make a chilli paste. Take 8 soaked dry kashmiri chillies and grind them with a dash of water and keep aside.
  2. To marinate the chicken we take 400gms of boneless chicken(cut them in small bite sized pieces) and add salt to taste, pepper powder a pinch, baking soda 1/2tsp, ginger-garlic paste, 2tbsp of maida, 2tbsp of cornstarch, 1 egg and mix it well. Keep it aside for 30min or more.
  3. Then we'll fry it in medium hot oil. When it's light golden brown color we take it out.
  4. Now we have to make the sauce. Take some oil in a wok and add 2 whole dry red chillies(optional), add some chopped ginger(2tsp) and garlic(1tbsp), add 1 chopped green chilli and fry it for some time.
  5. When the garlic starts to brown add diced onions(1.5 cup) and diced capsicum(1/2 cup). Toss it well.
  6. Add Soya-sauce(3tbsp), tomato-ketchup(3tbsp), vinegar(1tbsp), salt and chilli-paste(2tbsp) and the chicken. Toss it well and add 1 cup of water.
  7. Take some water and cornstarch and mix it to make a slurry. Mix it with the chicken and let it boil a bit.
  8. Add some spring onions as garnish and your dish is ready.
